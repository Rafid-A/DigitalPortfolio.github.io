ISP Project

This is an application is an error/bug-tracking alongside a planner/personal calendar, this allows software developers to plan their work, and also simultaneously track errors and bugs in their application and make a plan to fix them, who they assign to fix the bugs , and make a schedule of fixing them etc. This allows developers to have personal calendar and an work calendar with their team together to maximize their work efficiency and have everything in one place. This application is aiming to be developers go to application in the future to plan their personal life and while also managing their work schedule and tasks simultaneously.
