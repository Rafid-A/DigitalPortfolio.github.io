Bug-Tracker



Identity

We make one of the most advanced Bug-Tracking application designed for newer and emerging software developers.


Problem

There are a lot of bug-tracking or work planning software out there, but almost all of them are complicated to use and learn which makes it difficult for newer software developers to pick up the application quickly.


Solution

Our Product will be designed to help new software developers build their skills in a team environment and also help manage the team and effectively communicate with them over the internet due to the easy to use nature of the product.


Target Market

The Target audience is upcoming software developers but as well as experience software developers who are looking for a new management tools to help with their jobs.


Competition

We are part of a niche market in this type of planning and bug tracking application and there are definitely big competitons such as Jira, Monday.com, Clickup to name a few.


Revenue Streams

Our revenue streams would be through ads on our website.


Marketing Activities

We will communicate with our users through email newsletters and targeted ads on social media platforms and the internet.


Expenses

Our expenses would be running the website on cloud, renting database servers and to run ads to promote out product.


Team and Key Roles

Currently the only team member is the owner Rafid Ahmed. As we get started on making this product we plan to add employees in order to assist in making this product and marketing.


Milestones

As we grow, in the future we would like to be one of top choice for programmers to use as a tracking and planning software.
