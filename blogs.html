5-May-2023 Topic: AI Regulation

I am against Government putting regulations on AI. The reason why I say this is because I feel like the regulations that are set by the companies are already quite strict enough in my opinion. Government coming into this would only mean that the regulation on AI would become more strict which would really hinder the progress on AI as it learns from data and putting more regulation on it means it will haveless data in the end to learn from. Also there should not be a need to put a regulaiton on AI from the Government in the first place as AI has not advanced to the point where we need a legislative bod to come in and put laws and rules around something that is not even developed. The only things AI can do for the public at the momemnt that are significant is make their lives easier, not harded so I dont see the reasoning. However, I can maybe see some reasoning in why people would support this, but I think AI still needs to develop for a few more years atleast to answer where a regulation by government is needed or not.
