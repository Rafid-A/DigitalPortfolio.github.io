Space Attack

Concept

It's a top down 2D space shooter, where the player will upgrade their ship by killing enemies and attaching modular parts to it. The game world is infinite and its meant to be a Roguelike concept with limited amount of lives.If all lives are consumed then the game completely reset the progress.

Motivation

Gaming is something I really enjoy, and its a very big part of my life so with that in mind, I decided with my group to make a game as our ISP project. This allowed us to test our abilities on something something new and be able to grow as a programmer and expand our porfolio of projects.

Example Codes

This example above shows the how the AI/Enemy shootes the player. We needed to create a spot where the bullet would shoot from which is the Gunspot and also create a gameobject that is a Bullet. There is a fire delay between each shots that the enemy takes and when they take the shot a new gameobject is instantiated with the prefab and the position of where the bullet should come out of.

The purpose of the example above is to visually show that the player was hit by the enemy bullet. In this Code it take the spriterenderer of the playership which just shows the playership to the player and turns it on and off really quick for the invulnerablilty period that is set to indicate to the player that they took damage.During this invulnerablilty period the player cannot take more damage because in that time it is made so that the playership cannot collide with anything by changing the layer is being rendered on.

Conclusions

Since this was something new to our group we kind of set a low expectation for ourselves, so it went well for the most part. What didnt go well was learning C# while at the same time learning how to use the Unity Engine was challenging for everyone one of us, which meant it tooks us a while to get started which led us to having a little less time than we wanted. Time management was also a major problem due to us preparing for our AP/Final exams which led to us having even less time. This addition cost of time was shown in the game by out outdate looking UI & lack of content in the game.
