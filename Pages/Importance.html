The importance of Digital Portfolio

Digital Portfolio is important to me because I think this is a simple yet very creative way to show off ones achievements and it allows me to show my achievements the way I want to show them and possibly attract future employers to hire me. Digital Portfolios are important in general because they hold all your accomplishments, achievements, and work which it allows you to present it to the public so everyone can see how good you are. It is that one place where everything you worked hard on will be displayed and help you achieve the goal you were working towards. It is a digital storage of ones hard work in a sense. Digital Portfolio for programmer/developers is very essential and is a project in itself and is a very big asset to put on the resume. It allows one to get more essential knowledge and experience that they very well might need for their jobs in the future. It also demonstrates personal improvement over a span of time. A lot of people can feel stuck and this can help them reflect on how far they have come and give them more movitivation.For other jobs digital portfolio is still important because it gives them a place to display their work for their employers to easily see and encourage them to hire them.
